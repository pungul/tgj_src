Frequently Asked Questions

Following are list of frequently asked questions:

1. Who is YesGuruJi?
We are a passionate group of highly qualified professionals from diverse industries, with a mission to help young minds achieve academic excellence.

2. Why YesGuruji?
The management team at YesGuruji studied in small towns in backward states. They also went on to study in top Universities in the World including Yale, Kentucky, JNU & AIIMS. We were able to reach there because of the magic that good teachers brought.
We realize that ONE good teacher can make subjects interesting and easy to understand. YesGuruji aims to bring that good teacher into your homes, at your convenience.

3. What we do?
Our teachers hail from IITs and premier institutions, and understand concepts very well. After discussions lasting months, we have pioneered the YesGuruji method of “Video Lectures”. Follow these lectures sincerely, ask us your questions and doubts and master your subjects.

4. How it is helpful to me ?
Incorporating the revised CBSE curriculum for UP board, each lecture develops your interest and your thinking sequentially and rationally. Our methods aim to tell you stories on how you actually apply Physics and Chemistry in your daily lives, without the dry, boring approach of your books.

We understand you may not have access to the most expensive schools and teachers and coaching centers. Use these lessons to supplement your book lessons, and master your topics. Your teachers at school will like your new curiosity and understanding.

5. If I have a question ?
We believe the first sign of understanding is when you start asking intelligent questions. As you can see on the website, you can post your questions per the appropriate link. Our faculty will answer the questions as soon as possible, within 72 hrs.

6. If I want more details on any topic/ additional topics?
Comment on the Video/ Write in your feedbacks, with the specific point of difficulty. We will try to accommodate your requests as soon as possible.
