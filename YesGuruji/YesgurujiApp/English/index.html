Why Choose YesGuruJi

We are developing an interactive framework where the problems of the students will be addressed promptly and comprehensively by our experienced team of educators

Virtual Classroom Experience

YesGuruji provides a virtual classroom for the students. Our videos are as real to the classroom as it is technologically possible. We endeavour to connect students to the best teachers in India. Every student of India deserves quality teaching, and our resolve is to meet this challenge with the help of technology.

Best Teachers in India

We take pride in the fact that we have a pool of most talented teachers, educators and experts. The teachers are young and motivated with a desire to share their knowledge and help the students. They are not here to earn money, they have joined hands to pursue their passion and help the aspiring students. They come from the most prestigious institutions of India such as, the IITs, JNU, Delhi University, IISc, BHU, University of Mumbai and so on.

Chapter-wise Presentation

The curriculum has been systematically designed with learning videos based on each chapter of a subject. The idea is not to replicate the text book but to explain the core concepts of a chapter by breaking down the complex ideas and the structure. Our lectures are simple, comprehensible and interactive.

Preparing for the Exams

Our videos are geared to provide a comprehensive overview of the key concepts used in the textbooks. Our teachers have paid special attention to the concepts which are asked frequently in their exams. They have designed their lectures to prepare the students to answer the most difficult questions with ease.

Hindi as the Medium of Teaching

Keeping in mind the large number of students who do their schooling in Hindi medium in India, we have decided to create videos in a language which is used by the maximum number of students. Our mission is to provide world class education to the students of Hindi heartland who do not have access to good classroom teaching. We have used conversational language for our teaching so that the students can easily grasp the complex ideas. Simplicity of language and method is the hallmark of our online videos.

Innovative Use of Technology

We have used the cutting edge technology to produce high quality videos. We firmly believe that with the use of technology we can provide high quality teaching to every student in India. Our videos are crisp, to-the-point and interactive. We use graphics, animation and power-points to make the lectures as simple and interesting as it is possible.
