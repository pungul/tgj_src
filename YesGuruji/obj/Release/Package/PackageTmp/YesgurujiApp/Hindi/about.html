About US - A journey with a vision

Education is a fundamental right and every child deserves quality education. Good schooling is integral to the holistic development of a child. But in a vast country such as India, access to good schools is confined to a few privileged and fortunate students. A majority of the schools lacks basic infrastructure for teaching and learning. With the exception of a very few institutions of eminence, there is a remarkable decline in the teaching standards in both government and private schools. The teaching profession has failed to attract the best and the brightest as its mentors. There is a huge disparity in the demand and the supply. Our traditional pedagogical system is struggling to cope up with the demands of the aspirational generation of the 21st century.

Our modest attempt through the platform of YesGuruJi is to create an exceptionally high-quality learning environment for every student at his/her doorstep. We firmly believe that, with the innovative harnessing of technology, each student can get access to quality teaching and resources and fulfil his/her potential. We endeavour to connect gifted teachers to the students though online and offline platforms. We have mobilised a talented pool of educators from the eminent institutions, viz., Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), Delhi University (DU), Banaras Hindu University (BHU), Indian Institute of Science (IISc) and other such institutes. Our teachers are young and the brightest. They connect naturally with the students because they have had encountered identical problems as students a few years ago.

We do not seek to create an alternative to classroom teaching. Our goal is to supplement and reinforce the classroom teaching with virtual teaching, wherever it is required.

YesGuruJi is a professional attempt with an extremely diverse, experienced and versatile team of experts, come together to exploit technology to provide quality education for all at their doorsteps. The idea that connects us all is the ‘passion for education’. Our highest supervisory team consists of a professor, a medical doctor, an engineer, a journalist, a research scientist and a management guru. We are assisted by an advisory team for each subject. We have taken up this humungous mission because we believe that ‘change is possible’ and “we must be the change that we want to see in the world”. No weapon is as powerful as education to see this change.

Broad Objectives

  • Education for all at doorsteps
  • Innovative use of technology for teaching and learning
  • Smart use of virtual platform for interactive learning
  • Connecting talented teachers to aspiring students
  • Producing learning material for students
  • Teaching students in a language they are comfortable with
  • Establish a virtual platform for secondary and higher learning
  • Prepare students for taking competitive exams
